Welcome to the Canopy blog! We're excited to share our journey in building 
technology for the future of agriculture.

## What is Canopy?

Canopy is a platform designed to help horticulture growers make data-driven 
decisions. From small urban farms to large commercial operations, we believe 
that accessible technology can transform how we grow food.

## What's Coming

Over the next few months, we'll be sharing:

- Insights from the USDA horticulture census
- Best practices for controlled environment agriculture
- Case studies from growers using modern technology
- Product updates and new features

Stay tuned for more!
